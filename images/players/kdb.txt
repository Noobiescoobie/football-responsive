Kevin De Bruyne (born 28 June 1991) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Premier League club Manchester City, where he is vice-captain, and the Belgium national team. He is one of just three players (the others being Cristiano Ronaldo and Thierry Henry) to have won the PFA Players' Player of the Year award back to back, and the only midfielder and Belgium player to have done so in English football history
